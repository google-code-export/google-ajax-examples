Local Information

We're thrilled that you can join us in London for Google Developer Day at the Brewery.

Hotel Accomodations

The Brewery is centrally located in London, and near to a number of hotels. View these hotels on a Google Map.

Directions to the Conference

The Brewery is located at 52 Chiswell Street, The City, London, EC1Y 4SD (map).

The closest London Underground station is Barbican, on the Circle, Metropolitan and Hammersmith & City lines. Public transport information is available from Transport for London.

Things to do around London

If you are staying in London for a while, the city hosts a wide range of activities, for example the London Eye, the Tower of London, Tate Modern gallery, British Museum, National Theatre, National Film Theatre and the Institute of Contemporary Arts.
