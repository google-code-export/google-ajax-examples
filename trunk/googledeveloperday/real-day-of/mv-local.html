Directions to the Conference

From the North (San Francisco, SFO, Mountain View) :
Take 101 South until you reach the Guadalupe Pkwy (CA-87 South) exit. Continue on 87 South and take the Park Avenue exit and merge onto Delmas Avenue. Turn left onto San Carlos Street and prepare to take an immediate right after going under the overpass. Turn right onto Woz Way and take a left at Almaden Blvd. The San Jose Convention Center will be on your right.

From the San Jose International Airport:
Follow the signs to CA-87 South. Take the Park Avenue exit and merge onto Delmas Avenue. Turn left onto San Carlos Street and prepare to take an immediate right after going under the overpass. Turn right onto Woz Way and take a left at Almaden Blvd. The San Jose Convention Center will be on your right.

From all other locations:
Check Google Maps for directions from your location to the San Jose Convention Center.

Transportation to the Evening at Google

Google will be providing shuttle buses beginning at 5:30pm to transport you from the San Jose Convention Center to the Google campus in Mountain View. Buses will run continuously between the two locations until 9:00pm.

Things to do around Mountain View

There are plenty of things to do in the area if you're hanging around after the conference. Whether you want to geek out or just enjoy the beautiful weather, this area has something for you.

  • The Tech Museum of Innovation - Down the street from the San Jose Convention Center, the Tech Museum features an IMAX theatre as well as hands-on exhibits.
  • The Computer History Museum - Located down the street from the Google campus, the Computer History Museum has on display artifacts from the the early days of computing.
  • Foothill College Observatory - The computer-controlled telescope is open to the public on Friday from 9:00pm - 11:00pm. Head over on Saturday morning from 10:00am - noon and take a close look at the sun.
  • Santana Row - If you're in the mood for shopping, head over to Santana Row. With 70 shops and 20 restaurants, you're bound to find something.
  • Thomas Fogarty Winery - Located less than 30 miles from the convention center, this winery offers spectacular views of Silicon Valley, not to mention an award-winning Gewurztraminer.
